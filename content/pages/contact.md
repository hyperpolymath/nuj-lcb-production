## Workplace Issues

### If You Need Urgent Help

**For urgent workplace issues** (disciplinary, grievance, redundancy):

1. **Email** your branch secretary: secretary@nuj-lcb.org.uk
2. **Mark as URGENT** in the subject line
3. **Include** your workplace, issue summary, and contact number
4. We aim to respond within 24 hours (faster for emergencies)

### Chapel Representatives

If your workplace has an NUJ chapel (workplace committee), contact your chapel rep first. They can provide immediate support and escalate to branch officers if needed.

Don't know your chapel rep? Email us and we'll connect you.
