# About NUJ London Central Branch

## Who We Are

NUJ London Central Branch represents journalists, photographers, and media workers across London's central districts. We're part of the National Union of Journalists, the UK's largest union for journalists and media professionals.

Founded in [YEAR], our branch has a proud history of fighting for:

- **Fair pay and decent contracts**
- **Press freedom and editorial independence**
- **Safe working conditions**
- **Support for freelancers**
- **Equality and diversity in journalism**

## Our Members

We represent over 500 journalists working across:

- National newspapers
- Online publications
- Broadcast media (BBC, commercial stations)
- Magazines and trade press
- Freelance journalists
- Photographers and camera operators
- Sub-editors and production staff
- Digital content creators

Whether you're staff or freelance, experienced or just starting out, there's a place for you in our branch.

## What We Do

### Workplace Support

We provide:
- Advice on pay negotiations and contracts
- Support with workplace disputes
- Legal representation when needed
- Collective bargaining with employers

### Professional Development

- Free and subsidized training courses
- Networking events
- Mentoring programmes
- Skills workshops (AI tools, data journalism, multimedia)

### Campaigns and Organizing

We're actively campaigning for:
- Living wage for all journalists
- Protection for freelancers from late payment
- Stronger whistleblower protections
- Greater diversity in newsrooms
- Safety for journalists covering protests and conflicts

### Community

- Monthly branch meetings (online and in-person)
- Member forum for discussion and mutual support (forum.nuj-lcb.org.uk)
- Social events and networking
- Solidarity with journalists facing attacks on press freedom

## Our Structure

The branch is run democratically by elected officers who serve the membership. We hold:

- **Monthly branch meetings** - Open to all members
- **Annual General Meeting (AGM)** - Elections and major decisions
- **Special meetings** - Called as needed for urgent issues

All decisions are made by members, for members.

## Get Involved

### Attend Meetings

Join us at our monthly meetings to:
- Have your say on branch business
- Vote on campaigns and priorities
- Meet fellow journalists
- Learn about current issues

Meetings are hybrid (in-person and online) to maximize accessibility.

### Volunteer

We're always looking for members to:
- Join organizing campaigns
- Help with member recruitment
- Serve on committees
- Support new members

### Stand for Election

Officers are elected annually at the AGM. Consider standing if you want to:
- Lead campaigns
- Represent members
- Shape branch priorities
- Make a real difference
