### Get Help

**Workplace Issues**

- Contract disputes

**Welfare Support**
- Confidential mental health support
- Work-related stress
- Signposting to professional services

**Contact**: [welfare@nuj-lcb.org.uk](mailto:welfare@nuj-lcb.org.uk)
All contacts are confidential.

### Legal Advice

Members can access:
- Free initial legal advice (employment law)
- Contract checking service
- Representation in tribunals (where appropriate)
- Copyright/IP advice

**How to Access:**
1. Email [secretary@nuj-lcb.org.uk](mailto:secretary@nuj-lcb.org.uk)
2. Describe your issue briefly
3. We'll arrange callback with adviser
4. Turnaround: 48 hours (urgent cases: 24 hours)

**Emergency**: For time-critical issues (e.g., disciplinary hearing tomorrow), call National NUJ: 020 7843 3700
