## Purpose and Objectives

### Branch Purpose

The NUJ London Central Branch exists to:

1. Represent journalists and media workers in central London
2. Negotiate with employers on pay and conditions
3. Provide support and advice to members
4. Campaign for press freedom and journalists' rights
5. Promote equality and diversity in journalism
6. Deliver training and professional development

### Parent Organization Objectives

The National Union of Journalists aims to:

- Protect and promote the interests of journalists
- Defend freedom of the press
- Fight for fair pay and decent contracts
- Support members in workplace disputes
- Maintain professional standards in journalism
- Campaign for diversity and equality
